FAQ ❓

Questions people usually ask before trying it.

Clear answers help users and search engines understand what CogniFocus actually does.

What is CogniFocus?

CogniFocus is a distraction-aware focus app with a character-driven focus system, customizable timers, session tracking, and behavioral feedback designed to make productivity feel more engaging.

Who is CogniFocus for?

CogniFocus is for students, professionals, remote workers, neurodivergent users, and anyone who wants to improve focus, reduce distractions, and make productivity feel more interactive and supportive.

Does CogniFocus support Pomodoro and custom timers?

Yes. You can use Pomodoro, Power, Deep, or Custom modes depending on the kind of focus session you want.

What does Focus Shield do?

Focus Shield detects blocked social apps during an active session, triggers warning feedback, and helps pull you back before a short distraction turns into lost momentum.

Does CogniFocus track progress and stats?

Yes. CogniFocus shows distraction stats, focus score, and session progress so you can see how your habits are changing over time.
